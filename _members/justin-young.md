Justin is a Biomedical Engineering graduate from the University of Calgary. 
His focuses on the Calgary Bone Health Study (CaBHS), using HR-pQCT and DXA 
to determine bone health and architecture. 

Justin’s interest in bone and joint health stems from his involvement in soccer 
and is fascinated by the effects of microgravity in space on the human 
musculoskeletal system.
